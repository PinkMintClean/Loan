XVII. Legal Compliance & Studio Relations

To ensure 100% operational legality and protect UCM from copyright infringement, we have established procurement protocols with the industry's primary licensing bodies. This ensures every screening is fully authorized by major Hollywood studios.

1. Swank Motion Pictures

Function: Primary provider for "Public Performance Licensing." Swank acts as the legal gateway for the majority of top-tier cinematic releases.

Covers: Walt Disney, Warner Bros, Sony Pictures, New Line Cinema, and Lionsgate.

2. Criterion Pictures USA

Function: Specialized licensing for major independent and international studios, ensuring a diverse film catalog for themed UCM events.

Covers: Paramount Pictures, 20th Century Studios, and Blue Sky Studios.

3. MPLC (Motion Picture Licensing)

Function: Provides the "Umbrella License" for non-theatrical environments, covering a massive library of over 1,000+ rightsholders.

Covers: Universal Pictures, DreamWorks, and Focus Features.

4. ASCAP / BMI / SESAC

Function: Ensures legal compliance for all pre-show music and atmospheric audio broadcast via our FM Transmitter.

Covers: Global Music Performance Rights.

The UCM Compliance Guarantee

We do not utilize consumer streaming services (Netflix, Disney+, etc.) for public events. Every UCM screening is backed by a Signed Performance Agreement and a Commercial Exhibition Fee, which is built into our ticket pricing. This professional standard eliminates the risk of legal injunctions and establishes UCM as a legitimate theatrical partner in the Golden Triangle.
