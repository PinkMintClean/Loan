II. Release Strategy: "Zero-Tolerance Departure"

FM COMMAND & LEGAL ACCOUNTABILITY

UCM enforces a "Zero-Rogue" policy. The pasture is a sanctuary for livestock and families; it is not a rodeo arena.

01. FM Voice Call-Outs

The Operator will directly address any vehicle violating grid discipline. Public accountability over the FM frequency ensures immediate compliance.

02. Plate Forwarding Policy

Any "Rogue" activity (reckless driving, environmental damage) results in the immediate forwarding of License Plate data to local authorities for prosecution.

03. Mandatory Radio Lock

Radios must remain LIVE until the gate exit is complete. Failure to maintain the comms link is flagged as a security breach.

04. Law Enforcement Liaison

Local police are briefed on UCM events. Any individual endangering site safety or land integrity is met at the gate by designated security or local PD.
