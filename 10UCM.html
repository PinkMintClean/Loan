The "Drive-In" Conversion Funnel

How we turn a Facebook scroll into a sold-out pasture.

AWARENESS

User sees a 15-second "Behind the Scenes" clip of a popular movie trailer playing on the massive outdoor screen. The caption highlights: "The First Premium Cinema in the Golden Triangle Desert."

INTEREST

User clicks to view the Menu. In a food desert, the sight of "Artisan Sliders" and "Gourmet Popcorn" creates a secondary motivation to attend. They see the 60-day "Founding Member" discount.

ACTION

Mobile-optimized ticketing (Square/Eventbrite integration). User selects "VIP Luxury" or "General Entry." Ticket is delivered via SMS with a custom FM Radio frequency and Arrival Code.
